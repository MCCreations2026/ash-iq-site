Pine & Ash origin

Built by a cigar smoker, for cigar smokers.

Pine & Ash is the source brand. Ash IQ is the mobile app being built from that vision: a focused, private tool for tracking cigars and understanding your own palate.

The short version

Useful things usually start with a real frustration.

Ash IQ started with a simple problem: existing cigar tools did not match the way a smoker actually thinks about cigars. Review sites felt noisy. Social apps wanted a post. Loose notes had misspelled names and no useful structure.

The idea became a personal cigar tool: a mobile app designed around how real smokers track, save, and revisit cigars. Not a content platform. Not a feed. A tool.

The Pine & Ash name comes from the evidence left after a good smoke: scent, memory, ash, and the small details worth paying attention to later.

Mission

What we're here to do.

Ash IQ exists to give cigar smokers a private, well-built mobile tool for tracking their cigar life.

Your cigar preferences are personal. Your palate is yours. The way you discover, save, and revisit cigars should be designed around your own history, not around public trends or sponsored attention.

Product philosophy

How we think about building this.

Useful first.

Every feature has to earn its place. If it does not help you save a cigar, find a cigar, or understand your preferences better, it does not ship.

Private by default.

Your cigar profile is yours. Ash IQ is not being built as a social network, and your private notes are not content for other people.

Respect the smoker.

Cigar culture does not need a gimmick. It needs a better tool. That's what Ash IQ is being built to become.

Ship when it's ready.

We would rather delay a feature than ship something thin. The cigar world does not need another app that gets opened once and forgotten.

Version one

The foundation comes first.

The first release of Ash IQ is planned around a clean searchable cigar index, mobile profiles, cigar logging, favorites, want-to-try lists, personal tasting notes, ratings, and palate tracking over time.
