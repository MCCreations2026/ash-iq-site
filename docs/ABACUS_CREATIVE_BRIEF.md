# Ash IQ Creative Brief For Abacus

## Brand Purpose

Ash IQ is a future mobile app by Pine & Ash for adult cigar enthusiasts who want a better way to remember, compare, save, and discover cigars.

Pine & Ash is the source brand. Ash IQ is the public product.

The product should feel serious, organized, calm, and useful. It is a personal cigar journal and developing discovery tool, not a store, retailer guide, social feed, or cigar blog.

## Target Audience

- Adult cigar enthusiasts who want to keep better notes.
- Users who care about preferences, construction, body, strength, and flavor patterns.
- People who want discovery help without sales pressure, public hype feeds, coupons, or deal mechanics.
- Early testers who understand that Ash IQ is still coming soon.

## Tone

- Grounded, refined, readable, and direct.
- Calm, practical, and app-focused.
- Confident without gatekeeping.
- Avoid fake luxury language, cheesy smoke visuals, consumption pressure, or "elevate your experience" style copy.
- Avoid wording that sounds like tobacco promotion, purchase encouragement, retailer discovery, or lifestyle/status promises.

## Website Sections Needing Copy Polish

- Home hero: keep Ash IQ as the main signal and `Know Your Smoke.` as the primary tagline.
- App-focused value proposition: make the mobile value clear in fewer words.
- Why it exists: keep the Notes app/system problem specific and believable.
- How it will work: polish the mobile app loop without implying the website has app functionality.
- About origin story: add Gavin's real anecdote before launch if desired.
- App launch status: clarify beta timing once known.
- Contact/waitlist: replace pending public-form wiring after email hosting or a waitlist provider is selected.

## Suggested Graphics

- Final hero image: a real or polished generated Ash IQ mobile app mockup on a clean cedar/leather workspace.
- App screen previews: abstract but realistic mobile screens showing journal, saved cigars, and suggestions.
- Social preview image: strong Ash IQ mark, `Know Your Smoke.`, short coming-soon line, warm neutral background.
- Favicon/app icon: simple AIQ monogram or product mark that works at small sizes.
- Optional texture: subtle cedar, paper, or ash-neutral surface texture used sparingly.

Avoid smoke clouds, retailer shelves, price tags, checkout imagery, coupons, sale badges, or product packaging that makes the website feel like a shop.

## Suggested Launch Video Ideas

- Short 15-second app teaser: phone UI moving through search, saved list, review notes, and suggestions.
- Founder note clip: Gavin explains the problem with scattered cigar notes and why Ash IQ is not a marketplace.
- Brand motion card: Ash IQ mark, `Know Your Smoke.`, waitlist CTA.
- App walkthrough concept: one cigar record turns into structured notes, saved status, and a future recommendation reason.

## Suggested Social Launch Content

- "Ash IQ is coming soon" announcement with the public site link.
- Short post explaining what the product is and what it intentionally is not.
- Screenshot-style carousel: Journal, Save, Want To Try, Suggestions.
- Founder/operator note post from Gavin.
- Waitlist CTA that clearly says full app accounts are not available yet.
