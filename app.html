Overview

Your cigar life, in your pocket.

Ash IQ is built for your phone because that's where you'll use it: at home, in a lounge, or after a smoke when the details are still fresh.

Fast to open. Easy to log. Useful every time you need to remember what stood out and why.

Features planned for mobile

What Ash IQ is being built to do.

Search and log

Find cigars in the Ash IQ index or add them manually. Log what you've smoked with the detail that matters to you, or keep it simple with a name and rating.

Favorites

One-tap save for the cigars that earned a permanent spot in your record.

Want-to-try list

Save cigars you've heard about, seen, or been recommended before you forget them.

Tasting notes

Add personal notes to any cigar you log. Draw, flavor, body, finish, construction, and anything else your future self will need.

Palate profile

Over time, your profile reflects what you keep coming back to: wrapper preferences, strength tendencies, flavor leanings, and regional patterns.

Developing discovery

As your profile develops, Ash IQ will help surface cigars that may fit your preferences, grounded in your own history rather than sponsored picks or trending lists.

Mobile-only profiles

Your profile lives in the app.

When Ash IQ launches, you'll create your account inside the mobile app. Your cigar log, favorites, want-to-try list, notes, and palate profile will live in the app on your phone. This website is for learning about Ash IQ, joining the waitlist, and staying up to date before launch.

Discovery

Discovery that starts with you.

We're not building a recommendation engine that pushes products. We're building a discovery tool that starts with what you've already smoked.

The more you log, the clearer your profile gets. As this feature develops, Ash IQ can surface cigars worth your attention based on your palate, not someone else's ad budget.

This will develop over time. Getting discovery right matters more than getting it fast.
