What Ash IQ is

A cigar app. Not a cigar blog.

Ash IQ is a mobile app for cigar smokers who want to keep track of what they smoke, save the cigars they like, and stop losing the details that matter later.

It's a personal cigar journal, a favorites list, a want-to-try list, and, over time, a way to discover new cigars based on what you already know you like.

Why it exists

Because a Notes app isn't a system.

Every serious cigar smoker has the same problem. You try something great, forget the exact name, lose the photo, or realize your notes do not explain why it worked.

Ash IQ exists because there should be a clean, private tool for managing your personal cigar record. Not a social network. Not a noisy review site. A focused space for your palate, your notes, and your list.

How it will work

Simple. Fast. Useful on day one.

  1. 1

    Search or add a cigar

    Find it in the Ash IQ index or add a private record manually when the app launches.

  2. 2

    Save it to your profile

    Log what you smoked, add notes, rate it if you want, and keep your record organized on your phone.

  3. 3

    Build your pattern

    Over time, your profile reflects what you keep coming back to: wrapper types, body, strength, flavor tags, and origins.

Built for real use

The core pieces of Ash IQ.

Discover

Find your next cigar from your own history.

As your profile develops, Ash IQ will surface suggestions from your saved cigars, ratings, flavor patterns, and structured notes.

Favorites

One tap. Never lost again.

Save cigars that stood out so the name, vitola, wrapper, and your notes stay easy to find later.

Want to try

Your personal short list.

Add cigars the moment you hear about them, then check them off when you finally log one.

Palate tracking

You have a type. You just may not have named it yet.

Most cigar smokers have strong preferences they have never written down clearly. Wrapper, strength, body, construction, and flavor patterns all start to matter more once they are organized.

Ash IQ tracks those patterns quietly. Over time, your profile reflects what your palate actually returns to, based on your own smoking history.
